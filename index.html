Stop 06 · FAQ

Questions, answered

Do I need to install anything?

No — RouteRoush runs entirely in your browser, on desktop or phone. Turn-by-turn navigation hands off to Google Maps with one tap.

How are drive times calculated?

Routes are computed on the real road network, adjusted for time of day, plus a small buffer for parking and walking to the door. They're estimates, but honest ones.

Can I set rules per customer?

Yes — each stop can have its own service duration, earliest and latest arrival time, required weekday, and priority level. The optimizer respects all of them.

Where is my data stored?

Your customers and route plans are stored in your private account, protected so only you can see them. We never sell or share your customer data.

Do you support recurring schedules?

Recurring visit scheduling — "every customer, every N weeks, automatically" — is our next major feature, in active development. Start now and your customer list will be ready for it.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. No contracts, no lock-in. Downgrade to free whenever you like and keep your account.
